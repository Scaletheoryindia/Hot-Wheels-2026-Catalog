Categories. Each casting is filed once, by category: a Treasure Hunt or Super Treasure Hunt goes under that chase category, everything else under Realistic (a real production vehicle), Fantasy (a Hot Wheels original) or Pop-culture (a licensed fictional/screen vehicle). The card tags still show the underlying type, so you can see that (say) the Ferrari F40 Super TH is a realistic casting. Classification is auto-derived from name/series — spot-check and edit any you'd bucket differently with the pencil.

Data & photos. Names and series are from the Hot Wheels Fandom 2026 mainline master list (one entry per casting number). Cards show an original collector-card-style placeholder (casting number, series side-tab, a stylized car illustration keyed to body style) until you add your own photo — click any card. This is an original design, not the Hot Wheels logo/packaging, and the car is a vector illustration, not a photo of the specific model. Official/Fandom product images are copyrighted, so the catalog uses shots you take. 2026 mainline = 250 numbered cars; this list holds 244 (the source scrape omitted #243, #244, #247–250).
